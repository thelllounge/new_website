Welcome to my workshop.

This website will serve as two things.

First, a collection of Writing Tools

I have always loved to write. Because of this I have spent a lot of time doing it. There are a lot of tools I wished I had along the way. Sometimes my brain gets stuck, so I made a tool to force me out of that. Roll the dice and see what I must do in my story. Can't think of a title? Let the computer do it. None of the stories I'm currently working on are appealing? Let's think of a new prompt idea to write.

I have always had a bit of trouble with some of the sprint timers I've come across. A lot are based in Pomodoro, which is fine. It's a great tool. I wanted to be able to adjust the times though, and in the past many timers I saw didn't have that feature. They only worked with the Pomodoro style times.

Basically, I saw a space for tools I wanted to use either because I thought they would be helpful or because I thought they would be fun and interesting. (Like the title creator, I highly doubt it will accurately name your novel.)

I hope that you're able to find some joy out of these tools and maybe they'll help you to be more productive as well.

Second, a coding portfolio

During the beginning of the COVID pandemic I really questioned whether or not I still wanted to be a teacher. There were already a lot of problems in education and the pandemic just made everything a hundred times worse. I started to learn to code because of this.

I grew to love the ability to make my own tools and figuring out the problems that were in front of me. Each new puzzle stretched my brain in a way that teaching didn't and brought back a love I had forgotten I had for creating things. I also quickly remembered how customized I had made my MySpace in the past. Maybe I had always loved this and just forgot.

Until now, we'll keep playing around.
